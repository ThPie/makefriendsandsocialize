Code of Conduct

Membership is a privilege, upheld by the following standards. Please read carefully.

01
01

Mutual Respect

Treat every member with dignity. Discourse is encouraged; disrespect is not tolerated. We value diverse perspectives but demand civil engagement at all times.

02
02

Privacy & Discretion

What happens within the society stays within the society. Screenshots, recordings, and leaks are grounds for immediate expulsion and permanent blacklisting.

03
03

Community Safety

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, predatory behavior, or solicitation. Members must feel safe to express themselves without fear of unwanted advances.

04
04

Intellectual Integrity

Plagiarism and misrepresentation of one's status or achievements undermine the collective value of our network. Authenticity is our currency.
